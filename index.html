JADAVPUR UNIVERSITY, KOLKATA.

After i finished my schooling , I moved to kolkata for my higher studies. I was fortunate enough to get a seat in one of the top institutes in India. Jadavpur University has two campuses, one in Jadavpur and the other is in Salt lake. I study in the Salt Lake Campus . The environment here is pleasant and relaxing . The campus is rather secluded from the extremely busy streets of Kolkata. Currently pursuing my BE degree in Information Technology(IT), improving my web-dev skills and doing the best I can in academics. Its been 3 years since I joined Jadavpur.Away from home ,in this big city full of strangers I was perplexed about the idea of being alone. Little did I know that I was about to meet the most incredibly friendly people on the planet. The friends I met in JU, all of them are incredibly smart but if you have the perception that all the premier institues comprise mostly of geeks then I'm afraid , I would have to disagree .

FESTS:

While there's academic pressure in JU, there's also plenty of room for relaxing and having a well balanced college life. The academic calendar for the non-freshers starts in the month of July. The freshers hit the campus by August and two major fests are organized for their welcoming to their new home.(excluding departmental freshers ). "U-TURN" is held in the Jadavpur campus and CIIPUS is conducted in the salt lake campus, generally in the month of September .The month of March marks the commencement of the annual techno management fest of Jadavpur University ,"SRIJAN". If you are a tech enthusiast then srijan is the way to go for you. A month or two before the stressful final semester exams of each year, "SANSKRITI" is organized . Sanskriti is a prefect blend of western and culutural art forms . Various bands show up with their incredible peformances and the entire OAT roars in approval. Its a great time to be in JU during the fests.

MY TIME IN JU:

I would suggest JU to anyone, It's a once in a lifetime experience. I joined in the year 2018. Students come to JU from all over the country and its quite a diversity inside the classroom . You wouldn't know what its like to study in JU unless you experience it for yourself. Campus life is amazing here . The students in the hostel have a free internet supply, free electricity and many more, basically the government has done its share to help the students out financially .
